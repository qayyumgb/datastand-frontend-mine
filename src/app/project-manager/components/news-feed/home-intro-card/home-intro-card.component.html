First steps

Data Stand is a platform for creating, labeling and sharing datasets for NLP.

You can create your own datasets, or explore and reuse the ones created by the community.

Overall the platform is organize around three main concepts:

  • Corpus: a collection of unlabeled texts, e.g. "a corpus of 1,000 product reviews".
  • Label set: a collection of labels, e.g. for sentiment analysis: POSITIVE, NEGATIVE, NEUTRAL.
  • Task: a labeling task, e.g. "annotate the sentiment of 1,000 product reviews".
